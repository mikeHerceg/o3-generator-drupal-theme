    <h3>Usage</h3>
    <p>Social links are a group of external links to associated services pages. </p>
    <h3>Labelling Expectations</h3>
    <ul>
      <li>Each social link should either have an <code>aria-label</code> attribute or <code>u-sr-only</code> text that describes the context of the link.</li>
      <li>If a social link contains an svg or image, the <code>role="presentation"</code> and <code>aria-hidden="true"</code> attributes should be included on the element.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div class="documentation__col">
    <h3>Focus Expectations</h3>
    <ul>
      <li>Social links should have a visible keyboard focus state</li>
    </ul>
    <h3>Keyboard Expectations</h3>
    <ul class="documentation__keys">
      <li><code>Tab</code> = Move to next focusable element</li>
      <li><code>Enter</code> or <code>Return</code> = Navigate to the selected link</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
